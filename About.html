About

I am a Seattle based UX designer. However when I am not at work designing something, I can usually be found in my woodworking studio or at the climbing gym. My passion for design stems from an early desire to make things and learn how the world around me works.

As a UX Designer, I apply my automotive engineering background and product designer mindset to interaction design, usability/prototyping, and visual design. Working in both the physical and digital realms has given me a holistic view of design and an avenue into the mind of the user. By expanding on my foundational knowledge of industry-standard software, processes, and concepts I plan to continue my journey through UX as a lifelong learner.
