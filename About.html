My passion for design stems from an early desire to make things and learn how the world around me works. I was introduced to engineering and mechanical design in 2010 when I began an internship at a small machine shop, which led me to pursue an engineering in college a few years later. While I earned a Bachelor’s degree, I also discovered the world of product design. After college I dabbled in making shift knobs for classic cars in my wood shop before I finally launched my digital product design career. When I’m not at the computer I can usually be found in my woodworking studio, at the climbing gym, or wrenching on a vintage motorcycle.
